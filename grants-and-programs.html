What are grant and support programs?

Grant and support programs are available from across government to help your business. They can include measures such as funding assistance, mentoring programs and tax and tariff concessions.

What can I get help with from a grant?

Grants are designed to help businesses solve specific problems, such as providing funding to:

  • develop a new product
  • export goods
  • buy new equipment
  • recover from an emergency.

Keep in mind that government grants are usually provided for a specific purpose. If you're looking for money to finance your business, it might be more suitable for you to try sourcing funding through a bank loan, or getting an investor on board.

How do I get a grant for my business?

The process to getting a grant can vary. Generally, you will need to:

  • meet the eligibility requirement for the grant
  • invest time to write your application
  • report on how you spend the money.

For some grants, you will be required to match the funding amount with your own money.

Tips to apply for a grant

Grants or programs will generally have criteria your business must meet in order to be eligible. To determine your eligibility, you may be asked to provide information about:

  • your business’s annual turnover
  • how long you’ve been running your business
  • what problem you’re trying to solve
  • why you need funding
  • your customers or market
  • how your product or service can help to meet a specific need.

When applying for grants in general, make sure you:

  • address the merit criteria
  • show you’ve done your research
  • use evidence to back up your claims and reference your data.
