Next Generation Energy Storage ACT

Rebates for installing battery storage systems

Last Updated: 24 January 2020

Next Generation Energy Storage ACT

Next Generation Energy Storage provides ACT businesses with a rebate when installing battery storage systems.

Open

What do you get?

A rebate of $825 per kilowatt (kW) up to a maximum of 50 kW.

Who is this for?

ACT businesses wanting to install battery storage systems.

Overview

The Next Generation Energy Storage program provides rebates for battery storage systems in ACT homes and businesses. TheÂ program is delivered through the following battery storage providers:

  • ECG Electrical
  • RBE
  • Solahart
  • Solar4Life
  • Solargain
  • Solarhub
  • Sunstak

What are the eligibility criteria?

To be eligible for a rebate, your new battery system must:

  • be a new system that has not already been supported by the program
  • be connected to the electricity grid
  • be coupled with solar panels
  • include a new or existing inverter that meets the necessary requirements

How do you apply?

You can find out more about eligibility and how to apply at Next Generation Energy Storage.
